Map Scale Exercises

Ex.0

A chart has a scale of 1 : 1,000,000
How many nautical miles does 10 inches represent?

Step 1:
RF = CL / ED
ED = CL × RF
= 10 × 1,000,000 = 10,000,000 inches
Step 2:
1 NM = 72,960 inches
Step 3:
Distance = 10,000,000 ÷ 72,960
= 137.1 NM
Answer ≈ 137 NM

Ex.1

What is the scale of the chart?

Step 1:

1 km = 100,000 cm

= 1 / 100,000
